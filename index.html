FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers.

What kind of businesses do you work with?
Any business automating with AI — e-commerce, SaaS, agencies, real estate, financial services. If you do repetitive work that could be smarter, we can help.
How long does a project take?
Single workflows: 1–2 weeks. Full multi-agent systems: 3–4 weeks. Scoped upfront, no surprises.
Do I need technical knowledge?
No. Systems are built for operators, not engineers. Full docs and training included.
What AI models do you use?
Model-agnostic — Claude, OpenAI's GPT-5.6 Sol and Codex, Gemma, Llama, Gemini. Each task gets the best model for cost, speed, and quality.
What about after the project?
Every project includes a support window. Continue on our retainer or run it yourself with our documentation.
Can you integrate with existing tools?
Yes — CRMs, Slack, Discord, Telegram, databases, APIs, spreadsheets. If it has an API, we connect it.
